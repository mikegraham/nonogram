Data format
+++++++++++

Example:

::
  
  rows:
  2
  1, 1
  1, 4
  1, 3
  1, 4
  8
  7
  6
  4
  2
  
  columns:
  5
  3
  4
  5
  6
  6
  8
  1, 4
  2
  1
  
Notes:
  * rows/columns without colored squares are represented as a single 0
  * numbers can be separated by a comma, space, or comma space
  * not case sensitive; ``columns:`` can be abbreviated to ``cols:``
  * empty lines are ignored
  * ``#`` can be used for comments
